Home Exercise Guidance

Neck Tilts

  • Sit comfortably with your back straight.
  • Slowly tilt your head towards your shoulder until you feel a gentle stretch.
  • Hold for 10-15 seconds and repeat on the other side.

Shoulder Shrugs

  • Gently lift your shoulders up towards your ears.
  • Hold for 3 seconds and slowly lower them down.
  • Repeat 10-15 times twice daily for relief.

Single Knee to Chest

  • Lie flat on your back with legs straight.
  • Slowly pull one knee towards your chest as far as comfortable.
  • Hold for 20 seconds and slowly return.

Ankle Pumps

  • Move your feet up and down as if pressing a car pedal.
  • Focus on the full range of motion.
  • Excellent for improving circulation.

* These are general guidelines only. Please consult Dr. Munesh for a personalized exercise plan tailored to your specific condition.
